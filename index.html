Workout Warfare's board game

Workout Warfare's game with created to bring fun to the workout world while challenging your strength and skills at fitness. The Workout Warfare game is a game that you compete against your friends and family by completing multiple workouts and gaining points and bonuses. At the end of the game whoever has the most points wins the game!
